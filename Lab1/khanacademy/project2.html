Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 200 g
Eggs 2
Bacon 100 g
Parmesan 50 g

Steps:

1. Cook spaghetti in salted water.

2. Fry bacon until crispy.

3. Mix eggs with cheese and combine with pasta.

Caesar Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Quantity
Chicken breast 100 g
Lettuce 1 bunch
Croutons 50 g
Caesar sauce To taste

Steps:

1. Grill the chicken.

2. Chop lettuce and add croutons.

3. Mix everything with sauce.
